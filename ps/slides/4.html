Why Require.js ?

The Problems with the Multiple <script> tags are :

  • It’s just plain inconvenient. All the JavaScript in all the scripts is loaded into the global “namespace.”
  • When a browser is downloading and executing JavaScript files, it wil block the downloading of other content untill that’s done. This is why you’ve heard it recommended to put your <script > tags at the end of your < body >
  • RequireJS is one of the loaders, and we’ll get all the benefits of using it with AMD.
